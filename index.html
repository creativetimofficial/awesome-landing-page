FOR LOCAL RECYCLING HEROES

Recycling programs all over the world struggle to reach zero waste goals. Since recycling guidelines vary across different programs, confused citizens often contaminate recycling streams with non-recyclable items –or simply give up on recycling. More waste ending up in incinerators and landfills means higher greenhouse gas emissions. Use of raw materials instead of recycled materials in manufacturing consumes more energy and also results in more emissions.

Waste Warrior teaches citizens how to recycle according to local guidelines and highlights nearby recycling events and initiatives. The mobile game can be tailored to reflect local requirements, language, and cultural elements to complement recycling education anywhere.

PLAY THE GAME
